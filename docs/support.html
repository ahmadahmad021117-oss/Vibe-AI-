Frequently asked questions

How do I delete my account?

Open the Profile screen → Danger zone → Delete my account. This permanently removes your profile, food logs, weight logs, scan photos, and subscription record. It can't be undone.

How do I export my data?

Profile → Your data → Export as JSON produces a single file with your profile, goals, latest targets, food logs, and weight logs that you can share or archive.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Premium is billed through Apple. Manage or cancel anytime in App Store → your Apple ID → Subscriptions. Cancelling stops future renewals; access continues until the end of the paid period.

How does the photo calorie scan work?

When you tap Scan, VibeCal uploads one photo of your meal, a vision AI model identifies the foods and estimates portion size, then returns calories and macros. Estimates are approximate — you can adjust each item before saving. Free accounts get 3 scans per day; manual logging is unlimited.

Is VibeCal medical advice?

No. VibeCal is a general wellness app, not a medical device. If you have a medical condition, are pregnant or breastfeeding, are under 18, or have a history of disordered eating, please talk to a qualified clinician before changing your diet.
